Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: 2025

1. Introduction

Cancun Long Island ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you submit a private event inquiry through our website or opt into our SMS communications program.

By submitting your information or opting into our SMS program, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Full name, phone number, and email address.
  • Event Details: Event date, event type, estimated guest count, and additional details you provide.
  • SMS Consent: Your opt-in confirmation, the date and time of opt-in, and the method by which you opted in.
  • Communication Records: Records of messages exchanged between you and Cancun Long Island.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you access and interact with our website, such as browser type and pages visited.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your private event inquiry and check availability.
  • To send SMS messages related to inquiries, bookings, scheduling, reminders, and promotions.
  • To confirm reservations and follow up on event details.
  • To improve the functionality and performance of our website.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve functionality.

We may use the following types of cookies and tracking tools:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, such as remembering form data during your session.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics). These cookies collect anonymous data such as page views and time spent on the site.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your preferences and improve your experience on return visits.

Third-party tracking: Our site may use Google Analytics or similar tools to collect anonymous usage statistics. These services have their own privacy policies governing how data is collected and used. We do not use tracking for advertising targeting or retargeting purposes.

How to control cookies: You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies — please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect personally identifiable information for advertising purposes.

5. SMS / Text Message Communications

SMS Opt-In: By checking the consent box on our inquiry form, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Cancun Long Island at the phone number you provided. Messages may relate to inquiries, bookings, scheduling, reminders, and promotions.

Message frequency varies depending on your inquiry status and event timeline.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier rates apply to all SMS messages sent and received.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one final confirmation and then no further messages.

For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at (516) 200-3086 or cancunhicksville@gmail.com.

Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may inquire about event services without consenting to SMS.

SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist in operating our business (e.g., CRM or SMS delivery platforms), bound by confidentiality obligations and prohibited from using your data for their own marketing.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our business or others.

We will never share your SMS opt-in data or consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure data transmission, restricted access to personal data, and use of reputable third-party platforms with their own security standards. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. When no longer needed, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Unsubscribe from SMS: You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.
  • Cookie opt-out: You may disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
